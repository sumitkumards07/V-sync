# Privacy Policy for V-sync

**Last Updated:** April 14, 2026

V-sync ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the V-sync mobile application.

## 1. Core Commitment: "Everything on Device"
V-sync is designed as a client-side companion for students. Our primary design philosophy is data sovereignty. 

- **Academic Data**: Your attendance records, timetable, marks, and grades are fetched directly from university servers and stored **locally on your device**.
- **Credentials**: Your VTOP registration number and password are stored using system-level encryption (Keychain/Keystore). They are never transmitted to any third-party servers other than for authentication with the official university portal.

## 2. Information We Collect
We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) on our own servers. 

### a. Locally Stored Data
The following data is stored on your device and remains under your control:
- VTOP login credentials (encrypted).
- Local cache of academic records for offline viewing.
- App preferences and theme settings.

### b. Remote Features (Campus Services)
Features such as Mess Menus, Travel Buddy posts, and Lost & Found items are fetched from our secure cloud database (Supabase).
- This data is **shared/community data**, not individual user data.
- No VTOP-specific identifiers are linked to these requests.

## 3. Analytics and Diagnostics
We may use minimal, anonymous diagnostics (such as GrowthBook) to manage feature flags and understand app stability. 
- We **do not** track your individual identity.
- We **do not** sell your data to advertisers or third parties.

## 4. Your Rights and Data Erasure
Since all your personal data is stored locally, you have full control over it:
- **Wiping Data**: You can erase all local data and credentials by simply **Signing Out** from the App Settings or by clearing the app's storage in your device settings.
- **Access**: You can view all academic data the app has cached through the respective UI modules.

## 5. Third-Party Services
V-sync interacts with official university portals. When using the In-App Webview (VTOP Web), you are interacting directly with the university's infrastructure, subject to their respective privacy policies.
